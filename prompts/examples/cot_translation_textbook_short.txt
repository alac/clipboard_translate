ふむふむ (ふむふむ) [fumufumu] interj.

Definition: I see, indeed

つまり (つまり) [tsumari] adv.
Definition: in other words, that is to say

- つける (つける) [tsukeru] v. - to attach, to decide between

Definition: meaning that (casual)
- って [tte] - casual quotation particle (= と)
- 事 (こと) [koto] n. - thing, matter

Definition: for now, somewhat

Definition: passing grade

3. Idioms/Nuances:

• って事ね adds a casual and playful tone.
• 及第点 implies that while the answer is acceptable, it's not particularly outstanding.
